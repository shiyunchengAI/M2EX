The Suns picked up a third - straight win in this game and that marks their longest winning streak of the season. It was a hectic last 12 seconds, as the Suns scored the final five points in that stretch to win the game. Defense was key, as Boston was held to 38 percent shooting. Most of the Suns' damage was done inside, outscoring Boston, 52 - 32, in the paint. Isaiah Thomas carried Boston, as he accrued 35 points and five assists. Jae Crowder recorded a double - double, totaling 16 points and 10 rebounds. Eric Bledsoe led the way for Phoenix, as he accumulated 28 points, six rebounds and nine assists. Tyler Ulis was huge off the bench, as he hit the game - winner, while providing 20 points and five assists. Alan Williams also came off the bench to collect 11 points and 15 rebounds.
