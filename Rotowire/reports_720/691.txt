The Toronto Raptors (37 - 17) beat the Atlanta Hawks (43 - 12) 105 - 80 on Friday. The Raptors came out of the gate strong in their first post All-Star break game. They had a four - point lead going into halftime and then outscored Atlanta 28 - 13 in the third quarter and led by as much as 35 points in the fourth. Louis Williams came off the bench and had a game - high 26 points with four steals and a block. Toronto is 3 - 1 against the East-leading Hawks this season. Atlanta played a sluggish second half against the Raptors in Friday's matchup of the top two Eastern Conference teams. The Hawks were led in scoring by Paul Millsap, Jeff Teague and Kyle Korver, all three of whom started and scored 11 points. Korver went just 2 - of - 11 from beyond the arc, however. Atlanta has now lost four of its last seven games dating back to a pre - break loss to the Celtics. The Raptors will take on the Rockets in Houston on Saturday, while the Hawks will take on the Bucks in Milwaukee on Sunday.
