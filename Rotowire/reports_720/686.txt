This was one of the tightest games of the season, as neither team led by more than seven points. In total, there were nine ties and 14 lead changes, as it came down to the final seconds. Defense was key for Miami, as they held Indiana to 37 percent shooting and just 10 points in the fourth quarter. The Heat also dominated the rebounding and assists, winning the rebounding, 58 - 38, and the assists, 23 - 14. Paul George was the only Indiana player with more than 15 points, as he tallied 22 points, seven rebounds and three assists in the defeat. Myles Turner totaled 11 points, seven rebounds and five blocks. Hassan Whiteside was the player of the game, as he amassed 26 points and 22 rebounds in the win. The Johnson duo was huge off the bench for Miami, as Tyler Johnson and James Johnson combined for 29 points, eight rebounds and nine assists.
