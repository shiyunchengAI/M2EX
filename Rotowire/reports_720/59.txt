The dynamic duo of Gordon Hayward and Rudy Gobert led the Jazz, with the former supplying a game - high 30 points and the latter a 15 - point, 16 - rebound double - double that was his 13th in the last 14 games. Shelvin Mack and Rodney Hood also had matching 15 - point efforts. Joe Johnson led Utah's second unit with a modest seven points. The Utah defense stymied the Nets' attack overall, limiting them to 42 percent shooting and eliciting 15 turnovers. Trevor Booker's 17 - point, 15 - rebound double - double paced Brooklyn, while Brook Lopez managed 14 points, six rebounds, two steals and two blocks in a tough matchup against Gobert. Isaiah Whitehead went for 12 points in another start, and Bojan Bogdanovic and Sean Kilpatrick put forth a pair of eight - point performances. Rondae Hollis-Jefferson turned in 11 points in 14 minutes to lead the bench.
