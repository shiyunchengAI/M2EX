The Memphis Grizzlies (14 - 2) defeated the Portland Trail Blazers (12 - 4) 112 - 99 on Friday. Memphis has won four straight games and eight of their last nine. Marc Gasol paced the Grizzlies with 26 points and also dished out nine assists in 36 minutes. Six players scored in double digits, including Mike Conley who narrowly missed a triple - double by posting 21 points, 10 rebounds, and nine assists in 38 minutes. The Grizzlies are in the midst of a four - game road trip. They'll travel to Sacramento to play the Kings on Sunday. Portland had their nine - game win streak snapped with this loss. Wesley Matthews led the team in scoring for a second straight game, putting up 26 points in 36 minutes. Chris Kaman collected a double - double off the bench with 16 points and 11 rebounds in 25 minutes. The Trail Blazers will stay home to host the Minnesota Timberwolves on Sunday.
