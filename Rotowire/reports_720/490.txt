He scored 12 points, grabbed nine rebounds, stole the ball three times, and added one block to go along with a season - high eight assists. Seven of the assists came in the first half, as he helped his team recover from a 17 - point deficit. For someone who did n't play in the fourth quarter, the statistical line was all the more impressive. Over his past five games, he's averaging 18 points, 11 rebounds, 4 assists, 2 blocks, and 1 steals per game.
