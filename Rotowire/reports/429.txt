The Clippers had to play this game without Chris Paul and Blake Griffin, as this could be a sign of things to come. Minnesota did a great job of closing out though, as they finished the game on a 11 - 4 run. In fact, this marked their first win this season in a game decided by four points or less, as they had lost 10 - straight prior. Shooting killed the Clippers, as they shot 18 percent from three - point range and 54 percent from the free - throw line. Karl-Anthony Towns was a presence, as he tallied 37 points, 12 rebounds and five assists on 17 - of - 24 shooting. Andrew Wiggins was n't far behind, totaling 27 points, four rebounds and four assists. DeAndre Jordan carried the Clippers, as he dropped a career - high 29 points to go along with 16 rebounds. Austin Rivers was second on the team, providing 20 points and two assists.
