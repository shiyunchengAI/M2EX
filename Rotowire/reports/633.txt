While these two teams have been struggling recently, they both had one of their best offensive games of the season. Denver shot 56 percent from the field, while Orlando shot 49 percent on their end. There were six ties and 12 lead changes, as no team led by more than 11 points. Rebounding was key for the Nuggets, as they won that battle, 44 - 31. It was an all - around team effort for Denver, as they had six players score at least 14 points. Danilo Gallinari led the team with 21 points, six rebounds and three assists. All six of those players who scored at least 14 points shot at least 55 percent from the field as well. Kenneth Faried had one of his best games of the season, totaling 19 points and 10 rebounds on 9 - of - 10 from the field. Evan Fournier did all he could against his former team, tallying 24 points, four rebounds and six assists in the defeat.
